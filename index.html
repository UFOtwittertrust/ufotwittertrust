How to Participate

To assign trust to another Twitter user, tweet using this format:

#ufotrust @username +70

(assigns 70 trust points)

To assign distrust, use a negative value:

#ufotrust @username -30

(assigns 30 distrust points)

Note: Your trust ratings are automatically normalized to ensure fair influence in the network. There is no fixed trust budget.
